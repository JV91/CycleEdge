How the strategy works

1

365-day Z-Score — measures how many standard deviations BTC's price sits above or below its 1-year moving average. Positive = above average.

2

200-day SMA of Z-Score — creates a long-term momentum baseline. Rising Z-score above this baseline = building bull momentum.

3

BUY signal — when Z-score crosses above its 200d SMA. Enter position, ride the bull market.

4

SELL signal — when Z-score crosses below its 200d SMA. Exit to cash, avoid the bear market drawdown.
